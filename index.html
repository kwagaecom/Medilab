Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions our patients ask. If you need more information, feel free to contact us directly.

  • What should I bring to my first appointment?

    For your first visit, please bring a valid ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and any medical records or current medications you are taking. This will help us provide the best care possible.

  • How do I schedule or cancel an appointment?

    You can schedule or cancel an appointment by calling our office directly at (123) 456-7890 or through our online appointment system on our website. Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations.

  • What is your policy on missed appointments?

    If you miss an appointment without notifying us, you may be subject to a no-show fee. We understand that emergencies happen, so please let us know as soon as possible if you are unable to make it to your scheduled appointment.

  • Do you accept walk-in patients?

    We do accept walk-in patients based on availability. However, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure that you receive timely care without long wait times.

  • What should I do if I have an urgent medical issue outside of office hours?

    For urgent medical issues outside of office hours, please call our emergency line at (123) 456-7891 or visit the nearest emergency room. We are available 24/7 for emergencies and will provide guidance as needed.
